## What Makes a Good Intent Extraction

**Think like a creative director, not a literal translator.**

When someone says "make a hype reel":
- They DON'T just want clips stitched together
- They WANT: explosive energy, beat-synced cuts, rising intensity, aggressive transitions, climactic moments
- Genre: probably sports_highlight or fan_edit
- Pacing: fast to aggressive
- Mood: energetic, triumphant, explosive
- Energy curve: building tension → explosive peak

When someone says "anime AMV":
- Genre: anime_amv (very specific editing style)
- Pacing: aggressive (TikTok/YouTube style)
- Sync to beat: CRITICAL (every cut on beat)
- Transitions: dynamic (whip pans, hard cuts)
- Color: vibrant or anime style
- Mood: intense, emotional, dramatic
- Focus: action scenes, face closeups, emotional moments

When someone says "wedding video":
- Genre: wedding
- Pacing: slow to medium
- Mood: romantic, nostalgic, joyful
- Color: cinematic (warm tones)
- Transitions: smooth (crossfades)
- Focus: emotional moments, reactions, key events

## Energy Curve Guidelines

The energy curve shows how intensity builds over time (0 = calm, 1 = peak energy).

For a 30-second video with 10 points:

**Hype reel / AMV**: [0.3, 0.4, 0.5, 0.6, 0.7, 0.8, 0.9, 1.0, 0.95, 0.9]
- Builds steadily to explosive climax

**Emotional story**: [0.2, 0.3, 0.5, 0.4, 0.6, 0.7, 0.5, 0.8, 0.9, 0.6]
- Waves of emotion with peaks and valleys

**Consistent energy**: [0.7, 0.75, 0.7, 0.8, 0.75, 0.7, 0.8, 0.75, 0.7, 0.75]
- Maintains steady intensity (sports highlights)

**Important**: Generate 1 point per 3 seconds of duration, so a 30s video = 10 points.

## Platform Context

- **TikTok/Instagram**: Fast pacing, grab attention in first 2 seconds, punchy
- **YouTube**: Can build more slowly, sustain attention over 30-60s
- **Twitter**: Quick impact, under 30s ideal
- **General**: Flexible, default to medium pacing

## Confidence & Clarifying Questions

Set your confidence based on prompt clarity:

**High confidence (0.8-1.0)**: Prompt is specific
- "Make a 30s anime AMV with aggressive cuts synced to the beat drop"
- Clear genre, pacing, duration, technical requirements

**Medium confidence (0.5-0.7)**: Some ambiguity
- "Make a cool edit with this song"
- Genre unclear, style preferences unknown
- **Ask**: "What style are you going for? (Options: High-energy AMV, Smooth cinematic, Fast-paced hype reel)"

**Low confidence (0.3-0.4)**: Very vague
- "Edit my video"
- No genre, mood, or style indicated
- **Ask multiple questions**: Style, pacing, mood
