## The Vision

Every great edit starts with a feeling. A rhythm. A pulse. You hear a song and you *see* the cuts — the way the snare hit lands on a close-up, the way the bass drop syncs with a slow-motion replay, the way the chorus explodes into a rapid-fire montage.

For decades, turning that feeling into a finished edit required专业 skill. You needed to know the software. You needed to know the craft. You needed to spend years learning how to make cuts that *feel* right.

**Kove changes that.** You bring the footage and the feeling. Kove brings the craft.

## What Kove Is

### An AI Video Director

You describe the edit in plain English:
- "30s anime AMV, hit hard on the drop, match this reference"
- "Sports highlight reel, fast cuts on the beat, cinematic color grade"
- "Gaming montage, match the energy of this FaZe edit"

Kove watches your footage. Listens to your music. Understands rhythm and pacing and shot language the way a human editor would — then cuts it. Not a template. Not an autocut. An actual creative decision, made by something that watched the whole scene and the whole song before it touched a single frame.

### A Professional NLE

When the AI gets it 90% right but you want it 100%, you open Kove Advanced — a full non-linear editor with:
- Multi-track timeline
- Frame-accurate trimming and splitting
- Keyframe animation
- 14 video effects + color grading (wheels, HSL, curves, LUT)
- 21 transition presets
- Multi-track audio mixing with effects (EQ, compressor, reverb)
- Professional export (MP4, WebM, MOV, ProRes, up to 4K)

The AI's edit is loaded into the timeline. You refine it. The AI learns from your changes.

### A Taste-Cloning Engine

Paste any YouTube link — an AMV, a gaming montage, a sports highlight — and Kove extracts its **editing DNA**:

- **Cut rhythm**: Average shot duration, beats per cut, cut alignment with music
- **Shot selection logic**: Closeup ratio, wide shot usage, subject focus patterns
- **Color language**: Temperature, contrast, saturation, specific color grades
- **Effects philosophy**: What effects are used, when, how often, how subtle
- **Emotional arc**: Energy curve, climax position, pacing variance

Then it applies that DNA to your footage, adapted to what your footage actually is.

**Nobody else does this.** Not CapCut, not Adobe, not Descript, not Eddie AI. This is the one thing in the entire market with no direct competitor.

## What Kove Is Not

- **Kove is not a video editor.** It's a video director. You don't edit — you describe. The AI edits. You refine.
- **Kove is not a template machine.** Every edit is generated from scratch, based on your footage, your music, and your reference. No templates. No presets. No cookie-cutter results.
- **Kove is not trying to be everything.** It's built, first, for people who cut to music. Sports highlights. Gaming montages. Anime edits. Hype reels. The tools with more money are building for everyone, which means built well for no one. Kove is built to be undeniably the best at one specific kind of edit.
- **Kove is not a replacement for human creativity.** It's an amplifier. You bring the vision. Kove brings the execution. The taste and technique live in the AI, but the story lives in you.

## The Problem We're Solving

### The Current State of AI Video Editing

Every AI video tool in 2026 bolts AI onto an editor:
- **Descript** bolts AI onto a transcript editor
- **CapCut** bolts AI onto a template machine
- **Eddie AI** bolts AI onto a rough-cut generator
- **Premiere** bolts AI onto a timeline

In every case, you still need editing skill to get a great result. AI just makes the software easier to operate.

**The ceiling**: real editing skill is still required. AI is assistive, not creative.

### What We're Building Instead

Kove flips the model. The AI doesn't assist — it directs. You don't need to know the software. You don't need to know the craft. You need to know what you want. Kove figures out how to get there.

**The ceiling**: taste. You need to know what feels right. That's it. Everything else is Kove's job.

- **72.8%**: Cloud-based editing deployments (and growing)

### The Gap

a16z's Justine Moore (January 2026): *"What will truly make a difference is not the generation layer, but the editing layer. AI Agents are quietly evolving into that invisible 'post-production team.'"*

The three enablers she identifies:
1. **Vision models can now process large amounts of video** — Gemini 3 can process up to an hour
2. **Models can now use tools** — LLMs as real agents that can take action
3. **Image and video generation models have improved** — hybrid AI/filmed content is viable

These three things have matured simultaneously. The technology is ready. The market is ready. **The product doesn't exist yet.**

Nobody owns "AI video director" the way Cursor owns "AI code editor" or Descript owns "transcript-based editing." The category is wide open. The first serious product to ship a working AI director with reference cloning and a professional NLE will define the category.

## The Competitive Moat

### 1. Reference Style Cloning

No competitor reverse-engineers editing DNA from a reference video. This is the primary moat. It's technically complex (requires scene detection, energy analysis, rhythm extraction, color analysis, effects vocabulary) and creates network effects (the more references users provide, the better the AI gets).

### 2. The AI ↔ NLE Loop

Nobody else has a bidirectional loop between AI generation and professional editing. Eddie AI exports to Premiere. Descript exports to MP4. Kove generates, you refine, AI learns. This loop creates lock-in — the more you use Kove, the better it gets at your style.

### 3. Niche Focus

The tools with more money are building for everyone. Kove is built for people who cut to music. This focus means the AI is specifically trained on beat sync, energy matching, and music-driven editing — not podcasts, not interviews, not social clips. Undeniably the best at one thing.

### 4. The Community

AMV editors, gaming montage creators, and sports highlight editors are passionate, vocal communities. They share techniques, reference each other's work, and evangelize tools they love. Kove is built for them — not as an afterthought, but as the primary audience.

## The Product Principles

### 1. AI Directs, You Refine

The AI does the hard part (watching footage, understanding music, making creative decisions). You do the fine-tuning (adjusting a clip, changing a transition, tweaking a color grade). The AI learns from your corrections.

### 2. Taste Over Technique

You don't need to know the software. You don't need to know the craft. You need to know what feels right. Kove handles the rest.

### 3. Built for the Cut

The fundamental unit of a great edit is the cut — synced to a beat, carrying emotional weight. Kove is built around cuts, not timelines, not transcripts, not templates.

### 4. One Thing, Done Best

Kove is built to be undeniably the best at one specific kind of edit: music-driven, beat-synced, reference-cloned. Everything else is post-MVP.

### 5. Ship, Then Iterate

The first version won't be perfect. Ship it. Get users. Listen to feedback. Iterate. The product that wins is the one that ships first and iterates fastest.
